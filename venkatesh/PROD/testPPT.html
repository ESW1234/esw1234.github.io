Singapore Airlines AI Use Cases Go Live

A Case Study in Cross-Functional Collaboration

Salesforce Engineering & Customer Success Partnership

Overview

Challenge: Ensuring smooth go-live of customers in RED state

Solution: Partnership between Engineering and Customer Success Group (CSG)

Case Study: Singapore Airlines (SIA) AI implementation

  • Early collaboration to understand customer concerns
  • Joint approach to technical challenges
  • Knowledge transfer and capability building
  • Real-time support during implementation

The Partnership Approach

Collaborative Framework

  • Cross-team coordination: Engineering and CSG working together
  • Customer-centric focus: Understanding SIA's specific needs
  • Proactive engagement: Early partnership to prevent issues

Key Areas of Focus:

  • Customer concerns and requirements
  • Environment setup and access
  • Security considerations
  • Team coordination and communication

Four Key AI Use Cases for SIA

1. Assisted Service Replies

Automatic response generation via chat

2. Conversational Knowledge Search

Intelligent search of the knowledge base

3. Case Summary

Automated summaries of individual cases

4. Passenger Summary

Snapshot of passenger interactions across multiple channels

Cross-Functional Collaboration

When Issues Arise

  • Problem Identification:
    • SIA faced accuracy and latency challenges
    • Engineering team quickly mobilized to support
  • Joint Troubleshooting:
    • Engineers worked directly with SIA's team
    • On-site support at R&D office near airport
    • Gained access to sandbox environment
  • Knowledge Transfer:
    • Gen AI architecture fundamentals
    • NLP models and neural networks
    • Platform value vs. build-from-scratch approach

Technical Support During Implementation

Real-time Issue Resolution

  • Engineering teams available for urgent calls
  • Timely resolutions for quality and performance issues

Debugging and Access

  • Direct collaboration with SIA's Salesforce admins
  • Integration with ticketing system
  • Environment access coordination

Coordination with Professional Services

  • Clear separation of responsibilities
  • Prevented conflicts between sandbox work and PS efforts

Product Enhancement Based on Customer Needs

Driven by SIA Requirements

  • Consumption Tracking:
    • Detailed metrics on AI usage required
    • Solutions for tracking by feature and prompt
  • Feedback Mechanisms:
    • Built-in feedback for Case Summary and Passenger Summary
    • AI effectiveness measurement tools
  • Quality Improvements:
    • Root cause analysis for identified bugs
    • Implementation of preventive measures
