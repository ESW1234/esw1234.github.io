Experimental design:
  • showChatButton / hideChatButton short-circuit in CM deployments — emit onEmbeddedMessagingChannelMenuVisibilityChanged only, never touch the iframe FAB.
  • toggleChatFabVisibility drops its CM branch (now reachable only via launchChat post-handoff or non-CM).
  • businessHoursTimerCallback just calls utilAPI.show/hideChatButton — the short-circuit handles CM/non-CM dispatch internally.
  • Lifecycle paths (setIdentityToken, resetClientToInitialState, handleFabReadyEvent, handleMinimize) keep their cwcOwnsFab reads — these aren't customer-facing API entry points.
Compare against the canonical version at unauth-only.html, or the experimental plus-two unauth at experimental.html.
What to test (Unauth_Only — the only MIAW item in CM):
  1. Click CM FAB → click Embedded Messaging → modal opens. (cwcOwnsFab=true.)
  2. Call embeddedservice_bootstrap.utilAPI.hideChatButton() in console. Expected: CM emits {isVisible:false} only. Iframe modal stays untouched. (Old behavior post-handoff: would also hide the iframe FAB.)
  3. Call embeddedservice_bootstrap.utilAPI.showChatButton(). CM emits {isVisible:true}; iframe stays untouched.
  4. Trigger BH transition (mockbench or natural cutover) — CM gets emit; iframe untouched.
  5. Minimize the modal — iframe hides via handleMinimize (not via utilAPI), CM resurfaces.
