This page intentionally loads a buggy init.js to demonstrate what happens when the showTopContainer override in transferFabToCwc is removed.

Compare against the working version at unauth-plus-two.html.
Reproduction steps:
  1. Click the Channel Menu FAB (bottom-right).
  2. Click Embedded Messaging in the dropdown — chat modal opens, CM hides.
  3. Trigger a CM repaint by calling embedded_svc.menu.reorder(['EmbeddedMessaging', 'AppleBusinessChat']) from devtools console (or use the button below).
  4. Observe the bug: CM dropdown FAB resurfaces alongside the open chat modal — both UI surfaces visible at once.
  5. On the working page (with the override), the same reorder call leaves the chat modal in sole possession of the surface.

The 10s init-timeout watchdog scenario is harder to repro on demand but follows the same pattern: any channelMenu.js codepath that calls showTopContainer() after the FAB hand-off will resurface CM.
